IUH

The forerunner of the Industrial University of Ho Chi Minh
Univercity was Professionnal Training School in Go Vap and
founded December 1956. March 1999, the school was
upgraded to College Industry IV and December 2004 School
was upgraded to Ho Chi Minh Univercity of Industry under
Decision No 241/2004/QD - TTg of the Prime Minister.
